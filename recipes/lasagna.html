How To Make Lasagna

Ingredients

In order to make lasagna you need to start with a few ingredients.

To start you need meat, onion and garlic, tomato products, sugar, spices and seasonings, lasagna noodles, cheeses, and an egg.

Full Ingredient List

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Crushed tomato
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Water
  • White sugar

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheesee
  7. Repeat layers
