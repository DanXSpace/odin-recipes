How To Make Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

In order to make scrambled eggs you need to start with a few ingredients.

To start you all you need is butter and eggs.

Full Ingredient List

  • Eggs
  • Half and half
  • Kosher Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Black pepper
  • Freshly chopped herbs

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs with salt and half and half
  2. Melt butter in a pan
  3. Add eggs to center of pan and reduce heat
  4. Wait for edges to set
  5. When they are mostly cooked fold the egg into itself a few times
  6. Remove from heat and serve
